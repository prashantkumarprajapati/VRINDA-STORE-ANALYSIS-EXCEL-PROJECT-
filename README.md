# 🛍️ Vrinda Store Sales Analysis Report 2022 (Excel Dashboard)

## 📊 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive sales analysis of **Vrinda Store** for the year 2022 using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard provides insights into customer behavior, sales performance, and order trends across different dimensions such as gender, age, state, and sales channels.
The goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights using interactive visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
* Analyze monthly sales performance
* Compare purchases between men and women
* Understand order status distribution
* Identify top-performing states
* Evaluate sales channels contribution
* Study customer demographics (age & gender)

---

## 📁 Dataset Description
The dataset contains:
* Order details
* Customer demographics (age, gender)
* Sales amount
* Order status (Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, Refunded)
* Sales channels (Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, etc.)
* State-wise data

---

## 📌 Key Dashboard Insights
### 📅 Monthly Sales Analysis
* Tracks total sales from **January to December**
* Helps identify seasonal trends and peak months

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Gender-Based Sales
* Women contribute significantly more to total sales compared to men
  
### 📦 Order Status Breakdown
* Majority of orders are **successfully delivered (~92%)**
* Very low cancellation, return, and refund rates
### 🌍 Top 5 States by Sales
* Maharashtra leads in total sales
* Followed by Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Telangana, and Tamil Nadu
  
### 👥 Age & Gender Analysis
* Adult category contributes the highest sales
* Women dominate across all age groups

### 🛒 Sales Channel Contribution
* Amazon generates the majority of orders (~85%)
* Other platforms contribute smaller shares
